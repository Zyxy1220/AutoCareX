General Tips

Best Practices for Your EV

🔋

Charge Smart

Keep your battery between 20–80% for daily use. Only charge to 100% before a long trip.

❄️

Manage Heat

Extreme heat degrades battery faster. Park in the shade and pre-cool while plugged in.

🛞

Check Tyre Pressure

Proper inflation extends tyre life and improves range. Check monthly, especially after temperature changes.

🔄

Regenerative Braking

Use regen braking mode to recover energy and reduce brake wear — especially in city driving.

📅

Service Regularly

Even EVs need servicing. Keep to your maintenance schedule to maintain warranty and performance.

📱

Use the App

Monitor your vehicle health, battery and service alerts all in one place with AutoCareX.
